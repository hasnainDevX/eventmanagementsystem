FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our events page, select the event you're interested in, and click the "Register" button. Fill out the registration form with your details, and you'll receive a confirmation email.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the event. Log in to your dashboard and click "Cancel Registration" next to the event.

Are events free for students?

Most events organized by SSUET are free for enrolled students. Some special workshops or seminars may have a nominal fee, which will be clearly indicated on the event page.

Will I receive a certificate?

Certificates are provided for workshops, seminars, and training sessions upon successful completion. You can download your certificates from your dashboard after the event.
